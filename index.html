DG2DMM™

Data Governance → Data Management Maturity

DG2DMM™ is a pragmatic framework that aims to evolve data governance into a measurable, management-oriented discipline, linking policy, process and platform maturity to tangible business outcomes.

It helps organizations move beyond descriptive governance and towards actionable, verifiable data management practices that can be steered, measured and improved over time.

The framework builds on real-world experience with governance programs that struggled to deliver impact, aiming instead for practicality, focus and visible results.
